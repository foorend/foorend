
# Foorend Newsletter

## Overview

A single-page website for the "Foorend" food trend newsletter. The website will introduce the newsletter and encourage users to subscribe.

## Design

*   **Layout:** A modern, single-column layout with a dark theme.
*   **Color Palette:** Black background with white and accent colors for text and links.
*   **Typography:** A clean, stylish font appropriate for a magazine.
*   **Imagery:** A hero image related to food trends.

## Features

*   Newsletter subscription form.
*   Links to the Foorend newsletter and social media.
*   Client-side form validation.
*   A confirmation message upon successful subscription.
